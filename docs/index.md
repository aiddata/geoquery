# GeoQuery

**GeoQuery** is a free platform developed by [AidData](https://aiddata.org) at William & Mary that makes it easy to extract geospatial data for any area of interest — no GIS expertise required.

Select boundaries, choose from a curated library of datasets, and download results ready for analysis in Excel, Stata, SPSS, or GIS software.

[Get started with GeoQuery →](#){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

---

## How it works

1. **Select your boundary** — choose from existing administrative boundaries or upload your own shapefile
2. **Choose datasets** — browse datasets spanning population, climate, infrastructure, conflict, aid flows, and more
3. **Submit your request** — enter your email and submit; results are sent directly to your inbox
4. **Analyze your data** — receive a CSV, boundary shapefile, and PDF metadata file with full citation and source information
